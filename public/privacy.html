Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Anonymous Use

SmartScraper is designed to be a completely anonymous service. We built this tool with the belief that you shouldn't have to trade your identity for data.

No Data Collection

  • No Accounts: We do not require registration or login.
  • No Personal Info: We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies or trackers to monitor your behavior across websites.
  • No History: Your scraping queries and results are not saved on our permanent servers. Processing happens in-memory and data is cleared upon session termination.

Third-Party APIs

When you use our analysis features, content is processed via third-party AI providers (like OpenRouter or Gemini). These services receive only the website content you choose to analyze. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies regarding data transit.

Contact

Since we don't store your data, there is no personal data to delete or modify. For general inquiries about the application's architecture, please refer to our GitHub documentation.
